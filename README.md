# ARIA – AI-Powered Interview Assistant

> Making interviews fairer, smoother, and more transparent for everyone.

---

## Overview

**ARIA** (Adaptive & Responsive Interview Assistant) is an AI-powered assistant designed to address the challenges that candidates and companies face during the interview process. By leveraging artificial intelligence, ARIA streamlines communication, reduces bias, and builds trust between organisations and the people they hire.

---

## The Problem

The modern hiring process is often opaque, inconsistent, and stressful:

- Candidates receive little to no feedback after interviews.
- Hiring decisions can be influenced by unconscious bias.
- Companies struggle to evaluate candidates consistently across interviews.
- The lack of transparency erodes trust between employers and job seekers.

---

## What ARIA Does

ARIA acts as a neutral, intelligent intermediary throughout the interview lifecycle:

- **Guides candidates** through interview preparation with personalised tips and practice questions.
- **Assists interviewers** with structured question frameworks to ensure fair, role-relevant assessments.
- **Analyses responses** to surface objective insights rather than relying on gut feeling alone.
- **Provides feedback** to candidates after interviews, helping them grow regardless of the outcome.
- **Ensures consistency** by applying the same evaluation criteria across all candidates for a given role.

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🤖 AI Interview Coaching | Tailored preparation resources and mock interview sessions for candidates |
| 📋 Structured Interview Templates | Role-specific question sets that keep interviewers focused and fair |
| 📊 Objective Scoring | AI-assisted scoring rubrics to reduce subjective bias |
| 💬 Post-Interview Feedback | Actionable, constructive feedback delivered to candidates automatically |
| 🔒 Privacy-First Design | Candidate data is handled securely and used only to improve their experience |
| 🤝 Trust Dashboard | A shared view for candidates and companies to track interview progress transparently |

---

## Goals & Mission

ARIA's core mission is to **make hiring human**. We believe:

1. Every candidate deserves a fair shot, regardless of background.
2. Companies make better hires when their process is structured and consistent.
3. Transparency after an interview is not a luxury – it is a right.
4. Technology should reduce bias, not amplify it.
